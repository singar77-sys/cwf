ABOUT CWF

The most unhinged weekly wrestling show on Twitch - built from community members, inside jokes, and 6 years of stacked lore.

Clasper Wrestling Federation runs every Friday 10AM to 12PM EST on chimaira_mark's Twitch channel. WWE 2K26 Create-A-Wrestler universe mode. Every superstar is a real community member. Every storyline comes from stream lore.

The C in CWF stands for Clasper. If you know, you know. If you don't, start watching.

The SBW faction - led by the deeply wrong Bionix - is actively trying to take over the entire promotion and rename it. The CWF Singles Championship is held by the incomparable Cum Man. King Ardok shows up when he shows up and the building loses its mind every time.
